# Competitive Analysis: Gig Worker Tax & Mileage Tracking Apps

**Date**: March 25, 2026
**Location**: App Store

---

## Top Competitors on the App Store

### 1. Stride: Mileage & Tax Tracker
- **Rating**: 4.8/5 (88K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (ad-supported with optional insurance/deals)
- **Key Features**:
  - Automatic GPS mileage tracking
  - Expense logging with photo receipts
  - Tax-ready reports (Schedule C, IRS logs)
  - Health insurance marketplace access
  - General deduction finder (broad gig/contractor support)
- **Limitations**:
  - No auto-income import (manual entry or screenshot OCR?)
  - Less gig-platform-specific (e.g., no Uber/DoorDash earnings sync)
  - Cloud-based data storage (privacy concerns for some users)

### 2. Gridwise: Gig Driver Assistant
- **Rating**: 4.9/5 (26K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (basic), Pro ($4.99/mo)
- **Key Features**:
  - Auto-sync earnings from 20+ platforms (Uber, DoorDash, Instacart, etc.)
  - Hotspot/location insights for higher earnings
  - Mileage tracking with tax reports
  - Multi-apping performance analytics
- **Limitations**:
  - Weak expense tracking (no fuel, maintenance, or depreciation tools)
  - Less focus on tax calculations (quarterly estimates, SE tax, etc.)
  - Cloud-based storage

### 3. Hurdlr: Mileage, Expenses & Tax
- **Rating**: 4.7/5 (15K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (limited), Premium ($9.99/mo)
- **Key Features**:
  - Auto-tracking (mileage, expenses, income via APIs)
  - Real-time tax liability estimates
  - Speed/distance tagging, work hours logging
  - 1099 management
- **Limitations**:
  - Expensive premium tier vs. competitors
  - Less intuitive for gig drivers (more general freelancers)
  - Cloud storage (data privacy concerns)

### 4. TripLog: Automatic Mileage Tracker
- **Rating**: 4.8/5 (20K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (basic), Premium ($7.99/mo)
- **Key Features**:
  - Best-in-class automatic drive detection
  - IRS-compliant mileage logs
  - Expense tracking with receipt scanning
  - Team/fleet management features
- **Limitations**:
  - No gig-specific tools (earnings sync, platform insights)
  - Weak tax planning (no SE tax, quarterly estimates, depreciation)
  - Primarily mileage-focused

### 5. MileIQ: Mileage Tracker & Log
- **Rating**: 4.8/5 (80K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (limited drives), Pro ($6.99/mo)
- **Key Features**:
  - Simple swiping to log trips (manual/automated)
  - IRS-ready reports
  - Business/personal split automation
- **Limitations**:
  - No income/expense tracking
  - No tax calculations or deductions beyond mileage
  - Not built for gig workers

### 6. Shoeboxed: Receipt Scanner & Mileage
- **Rating**: 4.7/5 (10K+ reviews)
- **Pricing**: Free (limited), Premium ($19.99/mo)
- **Key Features**:
  - Powerful receipt scanning and categorization
  - Mileage tracking
  - Expense reports for businesses
- **Limitations**:
  - Expensive for gig workers
  - Weak mileage automation (no GPS trip detection)
  - No gig-specific tax tools

## GigLedger's Competitive Edge

Based on the website, **GigLedger** outperforms competitors in:

✅ **Privacy**: 100% local storage (no cloud, no data selling)

✅ **Gig-Specific Automation**:
   - Auto-screenshot import (DoorDash, Uber, Instacart, etc.)
   - Platform-specific earnings sync

✅ **Advanced Tax Tools**:
   - Real-time SE tax, federal/state/local estimates (PA-specific EIT support)
   - Schedule C preview + TurboTax CSV export
   - Vehicle depreciation (MACRS, Section 179)
   - Health insurance deduction calculator

✅ **Gig Workflow Optimization**:
   - Fuel tracking + MPG calculations
   - Maintenance logs with service alerts
   - "What-If" tax planner

✅ **Cost**: $4.99/mo (cheaper than Hurdlr/Shoeboxed, similar to Gridwise Pro)

---

## Where Competitors Win

- **Stride**: Free + insurance marketplace (good for new gig workers)
- **Gridwise**: Best for multi-apping insights + hotspot alerts
- **TripLog**: Best automatic drive detection (if you don't use gig apps)
- **MileIQ**: Simplest mileage-only tracking

---

## Recommendations for GigLedger

1. **Highlight privacy** as a key differentiator (many users distrust cloud-based gig apps)

2. **Emphasize gig-specific automation** (screenshot import, platform sync) in marketing

3. **Consider a free tier** with limited features (e.g., 10 screenshots/month) to compete with Stride/Google

4. **Add hotspot/location insights** (like Gridwise) if GPS data is available locally
